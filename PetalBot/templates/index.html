Iris setosa

"Iris setosa" refers to a species of flowering plants in the iris family, Iridaceae. However, in a more common context, "Iris setosa" is often associated with a specific species of iris called Iris setosa. This iris species is known for its distinctive appearance and is one of the three species commonly referred to as "iris" in the field of botany.
